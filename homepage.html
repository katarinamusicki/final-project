Serbian Student Association

The Serbian Student Association of Michigan State University was founded in 2023. Our mission is to create a safe space and welcoming community at Michigan State through the sharing of the Serbian culture. The club is open to any MSU undergraduate students interested in joining, even those not of Serbian descent.

The MSU SSA hosts weekly meetings where we discuss aspects of the Serbian culture, such as language, food, art, literature, and current events. We host a variety of club social events on a montly basis and welcome all guests! We show our appreciation for the East Lansing community through our monthly volunteering efforts.
